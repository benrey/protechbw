Okyo

  • Retail & Industrial Packaging
  • Manufacturing & Production Management

Palo Alto Networks is an industry leader in enterprise cybersecurity. They called on us to produce the packaging for Okyo, their first foray into consumer products. Okyo is a mesh-enabled router that secures all home devices from malware, ransomware, and phishing attacks. It’s a heavy device with a white surface that could easily be scratched during shipping without secure packaging. Our Fremont, CA team developed an environmentally-friendly packaging concept involving stacked corrugate that provided the same level of protection as a foam insert. The result was aesthetically pleasing and sturdy enough for direct-to-consumer shipping, ensuring that Okyo would arrive at customer’s homes looking great.
